AWS Pricing
- Pay only for the service you need, cost optimization
- No long term contracts or complex licensing
- Has management tools to monitor cost ofr better planning
- Priced independently and transparently based on-demand
- Don't pay while it is not running
- Has different pricing model, wither by hour or seconds, capacity reservation, spot instances, and savings plans

AWS CAF (Cloud Adoption Framework)
- Used to check cloud readiness for businesses
- Set of best practices, guidance, and tools that helps organizations digitally transform and accelerate their cloud adoption
- Provides a structured approach by organizing activities into six perspectives—business, people, governance, platform, security, and operations—to align cloud strategy with business goals
- The framework is built around a four-phase cycle: Envision, Align, Launch, and Scale
